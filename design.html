Through the eyes of the User

User Experience design consists of many different components stretching from psychology to visual elements. In this broad interplay there's plenty of methods to discover how a product or service is actually used. Ultimately, what we're trying to do, is to alter the flow, impressions and architechture into being more relevant, engaging and efficient for the targeted user group. Ombla can support you in the process of improving your UX stretching from User Research (interviews, analytics, observations) to implementation and testing (flow charts, wireframes, mockups, prototypes).
